About

SERA-X is a collaborative benchmark for evaluating emotional intelligence in AI systems—starting with text-based large language models and expanding to multimodal and human-in-the-loop systems.

Instead of asking "Does this model have emotional intelligence?", we ask:

Where is it already good at emotional work—and where does it still lean on humans?

  • Sense – Detect affect from text, speech, or multimodal cues
  • Explain – Infer emotional causes, appraisals, or social reasoning
  • Respond – Generate supportive, helpful, or context-aware replies
  • Adapt – Handle new cultures, slang, or emotional styles without forgetting
Each skill is scored independently, revealing strengths and gaps. A model may excel at sensing but struggle with adaptation—SERA-X shows where.
