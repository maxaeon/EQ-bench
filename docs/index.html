About

Welcome to the AI Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Benchmark Research Hub. This collaborative space explores diverse approaches for measuring emotional intelligence in AI systems, with SERA-X as one example. Join the conversation on GitHub Discussions.

Instead of asking "Does this model have emotional intelligence?", we ask:

Where is it already good at emotional work—and where does it still lean on humans?

  • Sense – Detect affect from text, speech, or multimodal cues
  • Explain – Infer emotional causes, appraisals, or social reasoning
  • Respond – Generate supportive, helpful, or context-aware replies
  • Adapt – Handle new cultures, slang, or emotional styles without forgetting
Each skill is scored independently, revealing strengths and gaps. Benchmarks like SERA-X illustrate where a model excels and where improvement is needed.
