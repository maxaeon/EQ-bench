About

Welcome to the AI Emotional Intelligence (EQ) Benchmark Research Hub. This collaborative space explores diverse approaches for measuring emotional intelligence in AI systems.

Instead of asking "Does this model have emotional intelligence?", we ask:

Where is it already good at emotional work—and where does it still lean on humans using different levels of interaction?

  • Sense – Detect affect from text, speech, or multimodal cues
  • Explain – Infer emotional causes, appraisals, or social reasoning
  • Respond – Generate supportive, helpful, or context-aware replies
  • Adapt – Handle new cultures, slang, or emotional styles without forgetting
Each skill is scored independently, revealing strengths and gaps, showing where a model excels and where improvement is needed.
